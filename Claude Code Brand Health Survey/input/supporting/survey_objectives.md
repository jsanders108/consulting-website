# ACME Strawberries Brand Health Tracking Survey — Q1 2026 Wave

## Study Objectives

This brand health tracking study measures brand awareness, perceptions, purchase behavior, and competitive position for Acme Strawberries against six competitor brands in the fresh strawberry market.

## Brands Tracked

1. Acme Strawberries
2. Crimson Crown Berries
3. Sunrise Berries
4. Red Valley Organics
5. FreshPick Strawberries
6. Golden Meadow Berries
7. Harvest Hill Berries

## Key Metrics

- **Unaided Brand Awareness (Q1-Q2):** Spontaneous brand recall without prompts
- **Aided Brand Awareness (Q3):** Recognition from a brand list
- **Brand Purchase History (Q4):** Past 12-month purchase behavior
- **Brand Evaluation (Q5):** 11 attribute ratings on 5-point Likert scale (Strongly Disagree to Strongly Agree):
  1. High quality overall
  2. Taste fresh
  3. Excellent sweetness and flavor
  4. Appealing appearance
  5. Good value for price
  6. Consistently good
  7. Widely available
  8. Committed to sustainability
  9. Attractive packaging
  10. Brand I trust
  11. Longer shelf life than competitors
- **Purchase Intent (Q6):** 5-point scale from "Definitely will not" to "Definitely will purchase"
- **Category Purchase Frequency (Q7):** How often respondents buy fresh strawberries
- **Acme Purchase Frequency (Q8):** How often respondents buy Acme specifically
- **Last Brand Purchased (Q9):** Most recent brand purchased
- **Net Promoter Score (Q10):** 0-10 likelihood to recommend scale
- **Brand Preference (Q11):** Brand of choice if all equally priced

## Demographics Collected

- Age (Q14): 8 categories from 18-24 to 65+
- Gender (Q15): Male, Female, Non-binary, Prefer not to say, Other
- State (Q16): US states
- Annual Household Income (Q17): 8 categories from Under $25,000 to $200,000+

## Sample

- N = 800 respondents
- Screened for fresh strawberry purchasers in the past 6 months
